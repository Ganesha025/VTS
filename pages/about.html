Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a leader in IT education

Founded in 2018

Vetri Technology Solutions was established with a vision to provide quality IT training in Surandai, Tamil Nadu, focusing on practical skills and job readiness.

First Batch Graduates (2019)

Our inaugural batch of 15 students completed their training, with all graduates securing positions in our partner companies.

Vetri IT Systems Launch (2020)

We established Vetri IT Systems Private Limited, our own IT services company, to provide guaranteed placements for our graduates.

200+ Graduates (2023)

Celebrated our 200th graduate, maintaining our 100% placement record and expanding our course offerings to meet industry demands.

Future Goals

Planning to expand our training centers across Tamil Nadu and develop specialized programs in emerging technologies like AI and Blockchain.
